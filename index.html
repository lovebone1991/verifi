What Verifi checks

Layer 1 · Structural

Is the model well-formed?

  • Formula errors (#REF, #DIV/0, #NAME)
  • Hardcoded values in calculation cells
  • Circular references
  • Inputs not separated from calculations
  • Toggle switches not centralised
  • Dead input cells that drive nothing
Layer 2 · Accounting

Does every number reconcile?

  • Cash flows balance every period
  • Debt drawn matches total repayments
  • Sources equal uses
  • Interest expense fully accounted for
  • Leverage impact correctly reflected
  • No ghost cash appearing or evaporating throughout the model
Layer 3 · Economic

The check that goes beyond the numbers

  • Leverage effect consistent with gearing and cost of debt
  • Yield on cost spread aligns with development value creation
  • Operating margin within sector benchmarks
  • Key inputs and outputs benchmarked against current market precedent
  • Among 30+ economic integrity checks
